Porto Pozzo and the surrounding area offer a selection of restaurants to suit every taste and budget. From the fisherman's trattoria on the edge of the fjord to the gourmet restaurant in Santa Teresa Gallura, including delicious pizzerias on the seafront.

## Porto Pozzo

The village of Porto Pozzo has several family-run restaurants where the fish is that morning's catch from the harbour:

- **Fish trattorias** on the waterfront with terrace overlooking the fjord
- **Pizzerias** with wood-fired ovens and local ingredients
- **Bars and artisan gelaterie** for breakfast or a sundowner aperitif

_Ask at reception for current recommendations on the most popular spots of the season._

## Santa Teresa Gallura (10 km)

Santa Teresa Gallura has a lively gastronomic scene with dozens of restaurants, from family trattorias to more upmarket venues.

**Must-try Gallurese cuisine:**

- _Zuppa gallurese_ (suppa cuata): the emblematic dish of the tradition — layers of homemade bread, meat broth and cheese, baked in the oven
