Conca Verde is located in one of the richest and most fascinating territories of northern Sardinia. **Gallura** is a land of contrasts: jagged coasts with breathtaking beaches, wild interior with cork forests and granite stazzo, authentic gastronomic traditions and cosmopolitan nightlife on the Costa Smeralda.

## Just Minutes from the Residence

### Santa Teresa Gallura (10 km)

The main urban center of the area, a lively seaside village with a characteristic historic center, craft shops, fish restaurants and the magnificent **Rena Bianca beach** with a view of the distinctive white cliffs of Bonifacio and the mountains of Corsica.

The beautiful **Spanish tower of Longonsardo** dominates the promontory and offers one of the most iconic panoramas of northern Sardinia.

### Palau (15 km)

Departure point for the La Maddalena archipelago (ferries every hour), Palau is also known for the **Bear Rock**: a natural granite formation that from a distance resembles the silhouette of a bear, which has become a symbol of Gallura.

### Porto Cervo / Costa Smeralda (50 km)

Italy's most glamorous port, famous worldwide. Worth a visit just to stroll along the dock in the presence of the world's most luxurious yachts.
