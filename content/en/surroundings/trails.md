## Walking Trails

### Capo Testa Coastal Trail (5 km, ~1.5 hours)

A magnificent coastal walk on white sand and smooth granite rocks from Rena Bianca to the Capo Testa Lighthouse (12 km from Conca Verde). The path offers views of the Bonifacio Strait, the Corsican coast, and the rocky profiles of the peninsula. Brings together historical, archaeological and natural interest.

### Valle della Luna Trail (8 km, ~3 hours)

A spectacular trail through the "Valley of the Moon" among boulders and granite formations that resemble a lunar landscape. Suitable for more adventurous hikers. The path starts from near Aggius and offers some of the most fascinating geological formations in Gallura.

### Juniper Forest Trail near Palau (4 km, ~1 hour)

An easy walk through the ancient juniper forests, with views of the archipelago and nearby islands. Accessible for families with children.

## Mountain Biking Routes

### Gallura Coastal Route (20 km, ~2.5 hours)

A scenic mountain bike route that hugs the coast between Santa Teresa Gallura and Palau, with incredible sea views and manageable terrain. Some sections are gravel; technical difficulty varies from easy to moderate.

### Tempio Interior Route (25 km, ~3.5 hours)

A more challenging route through the granite boulders and forests of the Tempio area. Suitable for experienced mountain bikers. A true test of technical ability.

### Porto Pozzo Loop (15 km, ~2 hours)

An easy mountain bike route starting from Porto Pozzo, perfect for cyclists of all levels. Beautiful views of the islands and moderate terrain.

## Tips for Trekking and Biking

- **Weather**: Summer is hot; best season is May–June and September–October
- **Water**: Bring plenty of water (2 liters minimum per person)
- **Shoes**: Choose sturdy hiking boots due to granite rocks
- **Equipment**: Helmet required for mountain biking; protection for falls recommended
- **Maps and Guides**: Available at the reception — we recommend downloading GPX routes
- **Best Time**: Early morning to avoid afternoon heat

Upon arrival, the reception staff can recommend routes based on your fitness level and interest and arrange mountain bike rentals.
