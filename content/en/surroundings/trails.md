For those who want a relaxing walk, a jog to stay in shape or for the most avid hikers, there are trails here for every taste.

From climbing on the giant granites of Capo Testa to paths reaching the most hidden spots, a walk on the Marmorata beach or along the seafront of Rena Majore are just some examples of the dozens of trails and excursions (including guided ones) that this corner of Sardinia can offer. Upon your arrival we will be happy to illustrate all the possible activities on site.
