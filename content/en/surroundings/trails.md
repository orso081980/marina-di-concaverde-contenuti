Whether you want a relaxing walk, a morning jog or are an avid hiker, there are routes here for all tastes.

From scaling the enormous granites of Capo Testa to finding the most hidden spots, a walk on the Marmorata beach or along the seafront of Rena Majore are just some examples of the dozens of trails and excursions (guided too) that this corner of Italy has to offer.
