Gallura has **nightlife for every taste**: from a quiet evening with an aperitivo to the most exclusive discos on the Costa Smeralda, through live sets in Santa Teresa Gallura bars.

## Sunset Aperitivo

The most beautiful experience of Gallura has no price: the **sunset aperitivo** on the west coast directly on the beach at Rena Majore or from the imposing granite cliffs of the Capo Testa Lighthouse with views of the Corsican coast tinged with red and orange.

Many bars on the Palau waterfront and in Santa Teresa center offer aperitivos with music and entertainment.

## Santa Teresa Gallura (10 km)

The center of Santa Teresa Gallura is very lively in summer, with many bars and venues open late in Piazza Vittorio Emanuele and on the overlook of Piazza Libertà:

- **Piazza Vittorio Emanuele**: the heart of local nightlife, with outdoor tables and live music on summer evenings
- **Bars, restaurants and sushi bars with breathtaking views**: In Piazza Libertà in front of the Spanish tower symbol of Santa Teresa and the famous Bonifacio Strait.

Events, concerts in the square and local festivals are organized every summer. Ask at reception for the week's event calendar.

## Porto Cervo and the Costa Smeralda (50 km)

For those wanting the **most cosmopolitan and glamorous nightlife in Italy**, Porto Cervo and the Costa Smeralda are easily reachable in about 50 minutes by car. Here you'll find the most famous and frequented venues by international jet set:

- **Billionaire Club** (Porto Cervo) — the most famous venue on the Costa Smeralda, frequented by celebrities and VIPs from around the world
- **Phi Beach** (Baja Sardinia) — quality aperitivo and music on granite rocks overlooking the sea
- **Ritual Club** — international electronic music

## Palau and Surroundings (15 km)

Palau offers a more relaxed and family-friendly nightlife, with restaurants and bars open late, summer concerts in the square and venues for young people on the waterfront.
