Gallura has **nightlife for every taste**: from a quiet aperitif on the Porto Pozzo harbour to the most exclusive clubs on the Costa Smeralda, via live music at bars in Santa Teresa Gallura.

## Sunset Aperitif

The best experience in Gallura is priceless: a **sunset aperitif** on the cliffs of Porto Pozzo or on the Santa Teresa Gallura headland, with the Corsican coast turning red and orange.

## Santa Teresa Gallura (10 km)

Santa Teresa Gallura is very lively in summer, with bars and venues open late in Piazza Vittorio Emanuele and on the seafront.

## Porto Cervo and the Costa Smeralda (50 km)

For the most **cosmopolitan and glamorous nightlife in Italy**, Porto Cervo and the Costa Smeralda are easily reachable in about 50 minutes. Here you'll find the most famous clubs frequented by the international jet set:

- **Billionaire Club** (Porto Cervo)
- **Phi Beach** (Baja Sardinia) — aperitif and quality music on granite rocks overlooking the sea
- **Ritual Club** — international electronic music
