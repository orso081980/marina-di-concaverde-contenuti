Porto Pozzo is in an **extraordinarily privileged position**: within a 50 km radius lie some of the world's most famous and beautiful tourist destinations.

## La Maddalena Archipelago

A National Park of extraordinary natural beauty: 27 islands and islets with crystal-clear water, pink sand beaches and unique wildlife.

## Bonifacio, Corsica

Just 20 minutes by ferry from Santa Teresa Gallura, Bonifacio is a stunning medieval clifftop town — one of the most dramatic in the whole Mediterranean.

## Costa Smeralda

World-famous for glamour and natural beauty: Porto Cervo, Baia Sardinia and Cala di Volpe are just some of the emblematic spots of this legendary coast.
