Conca Verde is in an **extraordinarily privileged position**: within a 35 km radius you'll find most of the world's most famous and beautiful tourist destinations. Here are our top recommendations.

## 🏝 La Maddalena Archipelago

The **National Park of the La Maddalena Archipelago** is the number one destination. Accessible in just 15 minutes by dinghy directly from Conca Verde or 15 minutes by ferry from Palau, the archipelago includes 7 main islands and dozens of islets, with emerald turquoise waters and white sand beaches.

Islands to visit:

- **La Maddalena** (main island): lively historic center populated by restaurants for all tastes, picturesque port and numerous beaches accessible by car or renting a bicycle.
- **Caprera**: Garibaldi's island, with his house-museum and wild coves like Cala Coticcio.
- **Spargi**: the beach of Cala Corsara, considered one of the most beautiful in Italy
- **Budelli**: the legendary **Pink Beach**, colored by the remains of corals and shells (visit from water only — landing prohibited)
- **Santa Maria** and **Razzoli**: the northernmost islands, crystal-clear waters and whitest sand.

## 🏰 Bonifacio, Corsica (50 min by ferry)

From the port of Santa Teresa Gallura (10 km from the residence) ferries depart to **Bonifacio**, Corsica's most picturesque city. Perched on white limestone cliffs towering over the sea, Bonifacio is a true masterpiece of nature.

Must-see in Bonifacio:

- The medieval **Citadel** and its narrow streets
- The **Bonifacio Cliffs** and marine caves
- The **Scala du Rei d'Aragona** (187 steps carved into the rock)
- The **Roman cisterns** in the heart of the city

## 🌊 Northern Beaches

Gallura is famous for its beaches: very fine white sand, clear waters, pink granite rocks. Some must-sees near the residence:

- **Rena Bianca** (Santa Teresa Gallura, 10 km) — the town beach with views of Bonifacio
- **Capo Testa** (Santa Teresa Gallura, 12 km) — Peninsula with 3 beaches, each more beautiful than the last and the mythical Capo Testa Lighthouse among wind-sculpted granite rocks
- **Valle dell'erica** (Porto Pozzo, 4 km) — small gem with archipelago views just 5 km from Conca Verde.
- **Rena Majore** On the west coast 15 min by car from Conca Verde this beach dominated by extensive sand dunes offers sunsets that stop time.

## 🏎 Porto Cervo and the Costa Smeralda (50 km)

Italy's most exclusive port, born in the 1960s from the vision of the Aga Khan. Luxury boutiques, yachts and Mediterranean charm make Porto Cervo a unique destination. Worth a visit just for a stroll.

## 🏔 Tempio Pausania and the Gallurese Interior (50 km)

For those who love the interior and culture, **Tempio Pausania** is the capital of historic Gallura: well-preserved medieval center, granite alleys, stone fountains and the famous **Tempio Carnival** (off-season). Perfect half-day trip by car.
