In Santa Teresa Gallura we work with an excellent **family-run NCC company** offering a professional, punctual and very competitive service. Whether you need an economy car for a day, a taxi to Porto Cervo or a transfer to and from the airport: we will always find the best solution.

## Transfer to/from Olbia Airport

Olbia–Costa Smeralda airport is approximately **35 minutes** from the residence. If you are flying in, organise the transfer before you travel: we will wait for you at the baggage exit with a sign bearing your name and take you directly to the residence, without stress and without waiting for scheduled buses.

The service is also available **24 hours a day** for late-arriving flights.

## Car Hire

If you prefer to travel independently, we can book you a hire car on site at **special rates reserved** for residence guests. Economy cars, SUVs, vans and scooters: the fleet covers all needs.
