The bicycle is undoubtedly one of the **best ways to connect with nature** and explore the most unspoilt and beautiful areas of Gallura. From panoramic coastal trails to dirt tracks inland, every pedal stroke becomes a visual adventure.

## Mountain Bike Hire

At the **residence reception**, trekking and front-suspension mountain bikes are available for hire. Bikes are regularly maintained and provided with helmet, lock and repair kit.

| Full day           | € 22 |

_15% discount for bookings of 5 or more consecutive days._

## Main Routes

### Fjord Circuit (12 km – easy)

Starting from the residence, the route follows the Porto Pozzo fjord along white roads and well-marked trails. Flat, suitable for everyone on any type of bicycle. Spectacular views of the La Maddalena Archipelago.

**Estimated time:** 1 h 30 min | **Elevation:** 50 m
