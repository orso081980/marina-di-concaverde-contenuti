Marina di Conca Verde is nestled in the natural fjord of **Porto Pozzo**, one of the most sheltered and crystal-clear corners of northern Sardinia. The residence overlooks the fjord with a private beach, just a few minutes by boat from the **La Maddalena Archipelago National Park**.
