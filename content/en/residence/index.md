Marina di Conca Verde residence is a small seaside village composed of villas in typical "Stazzu" Gallurese style, completely covered in Sardinian granite and immersed in vegetation. Clean and tidy furnishings, maritime style, with built-in wardrobes and solid wood paneling.

Located in Conca Verde, a small residential area halfway between Santa Teresa Gallura and Palau, directly across from the islands of the La Maddalena Archipelago reachable in 15 minutes by renting a dinghy directly from the beach.

The characteristic coves are 300m away. For those wanting more, the equipped beach with sun loungers, umbrellas and beach bar is 1 km away, easily walkable along the quiet Conca Verde seafront.
