The **Conca Verde** fjord is one of the most evocative corners of the entire Sardinian coast. The waters, protected by the natural shape of the bay, range from deep blue to emerald green, with shallow and flat seabeds — ideal for families with children.

## The Equipped Beach

Our private beach, reserved for residence guests, offers:

- **Sun loungers and parasols** included in the stay
- **Showers** with fresh water on the beach
- **Beach bar** with snacks, aperitifs and cold drinks
- **Canoe and kayak hire** to explore nearby coves
- **Dinghy hire** to independently reach the La Maddalena Archipelago

## The Coves of Conca Verde

The fjord features numerous **uncrowded little coves**, hidden by Mediterranean vegetation. Mucchi Bianchi, Cala Giunco, Cala Stagnali: each cove has its own charm, with crystal-clear water and fine sand. Many are only accessible by sea — all the more reason to hire a dinghy!

## The Islands of the Archipelago

Just **10 minutes by dinghy** you can reach the wonderful islands of the **La Maddalena Archipelago**: Spargi, Budelli with the famous pink beach, Santa Maria, Razzoli. A true Gallurese paradise, these islands are protected within the National Park and offer breathtaking scenery.
