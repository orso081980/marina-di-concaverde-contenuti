**Sardinia is reachable by sea** from several ports on the Italian mainland. The nearest port to the residence is **Olbia** (60 km), served year-round by frequent connections. For those already in Sardinia, **Santa Teresa Gallura** (10 km from the residence) has ferries to **Bonifacio** in Corsica.

## Ferries to Olbia — To/from the Mainland

Olbia is the main arrival port for northern Sardinia. The main operators and active routes:

### Moby Lines

- **Livorno–Olbia**: daily departures, duration approximately 7–8 hours (overnight) or 5 hours (daytime)
- **Civitavecchia–Olbia**: frequent daily departures, duration approximately 7–8 hours
- **Genoa–Olbia**: overnight route, duration approximately 13–14 hours

### Tirrenia

- **Civitavecchia–Olbia**: various daily departures, duration 7–8 hours
- **Genoa–Olbia**: summer overnight service, duration 13 hours

### GNV – Grandi Navi Veloci

- **Civitavecchia–Olbia**: summer service
- **Genoa–Olbia**: summer overnight service

### Grimaldi Lines

- **Civitavecchia–Porto Torres**: alternative to Olbia port (Porto Torres is 100 km from the residence)

## Ferries to Porto Torres — Alternative

**Porto Torres** (100 km from the residence) is served by:

- **Moby Lines** from Genoa
- **Tirrenia** from Genoa and Civitavecchia
- **GNV** from Genoa
- **Corsica Sardinia Ferries** from Toulon (France)

## Santa Teresa Gallura–Bonifacio (Corsica)

To reach **Bonifacio** in Corsica (the baroque city perched on white cliffs) just **50 minutes of ferry** from Santa Teresa Gallura port (10 km from the residence).

- [traghetti.com](https://www.traghetti.com) — all companies comparison
- [Moby Lines](https://www.moby.it) — direct booking
- [Tirrenia](https://www.tirrenia.it) — direct booking
- [GNV](https://www.gnv.it) — direct booking

## From Olbia Airport to Residence

If you prefer flying, **Olbia–Costa Smeralda Airport** is just 60 minutes from the residence.
