**Sardinia is reachable by sea** from several ports on the Italian mainland. The nearest port to the residence is **Olbia** (35 km), served year-round by frequent connections. For those already in Sardinia, from **Santa Teresa Gallura** (10 km from the residence) fast ferries depart for **Bonifacio** in Corsica.

## Ferries to Olbia — To/from the Mainland

Olbia is the main arrival port for northern Sardinia. Main operators and active routes:

### Moby Lines

- **Livorno–Olbia**: daily departures, approx. 7–8 hours (night) or 5 hours (day)
- **Civitavecchia–Olbia**: frequent departures, approx. 7–8 hours
- **Genoa–Olbia**: overnight route, approx. 13–14 hours

### Tirrenia

- **Civitavecchia–Olbia**: various daily departures, 7–8 hours
- **Genoa–Olbia**: summer overnight service, 13 hours

### GNV – Grandi Navi Veloci

- **Civitavecchia–Olbia**: summer service
- **Genoa–Olbia**: summer overnight service

### Grimaldi Lines

- **Civitavecchia–Porto Torres**: alternative to Olbia port (Porto Torres is 90 km from the residence)
