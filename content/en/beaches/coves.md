The **Porto Pozzo** fjord is a picturesque corner of the Gallurese coast. The waters, protected by the natural conformation of the bay, are a safe destination for those who don't want to give up the sea even on the fateful days of the Maestrale wind. With shallow and flat bottoms — ideal for families with children and for those who want to enjoy the sea in total tranquility.
