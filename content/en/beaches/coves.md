The **Conca Verde** fjord is one of the most evocative corners of the entire Sardinian coast. The waters, protected by the natural shape of the bay, range from deep blue to emerald green, with shallow and flat seabeds — ideal for families with children and for those who want to enjoy the sea in complete tranquillity.

## The Residence Beach

Our private beach, reserved for residence guests, offers:

- **Sun loungers and parasols** included in the stay
- **Showers** with fresh water on the beach
- **Beach bar** with snacks, aperitifs and cold drinks
- **Canoe and kayak hire** to explore nearby coves
- **Dinghy hire** to independently reach the islands

## The Fjord Coves

The fjord is dotted with numerous **uncrowded little coves**, hidden by Mediterranean scrubland. Each has its own character:

### Mucchi Bianchi

The most famous cove in Porto Pozzo, with its characteristic white granite rocks emerging from the turquoise water. Reachable on foot or by dinghy.

### Cala Giunco

A small inlet sheltered by vegetation, with shallow seabeds and fine sand. Perfect for children.

### Cala Stagnali

More secluded and less frequented, mainly reachable by sea. Crystal-clear waters and an unspoilt atmosphere.

Many coves are only reachable **by sea** — all the more reason to hire a dinghy from reception!
