Located on the natural fjord of Porto Pozzo, right across from the La Maddalena Archipelago and the island of Coluccia, it offers welcoming villas in typical "Stazzu" Gallurese style with exposed Sardinian granite and solid wood.

With over 40 million years of geological history, this fjord and its conca (green) are mentioned in Homer's Odyssey. Here Ulysses encountered the so-called Laestrygonians. Today Porto Pozzo is a pleasant fishing village that welcomes us with its small family shops, fish market, restaurants and pizzerias, bars, artisan gelato parlor, pharmacy and minimarket.

Moreover, its well-protected marina offers an ideal starting point for discovering the archipelago and its enchanting beaches.
